② Brand voice — how Skyquire sounds

🎙️ Voice in one breath

Clear, credible, and a little contrarian. You're the sharp operator who demystifies M&A for normal people — not a suit, not a hype-man. Teach first, sell second. Indian context always (₹, MSME, GST, tier-2 cities, real examples).

DialWhere Skyquire sits
Formal ↔ Casual70% casual — like explaining to a smart friend
Serious ↔ PlayfulMostly serious, occasional wit
Jargon ↔ PlainPlain. Define any M&A term you use
Humble ↔ BoldBold on the mission, humble on the journey

✅ Do / 🚫 Don't

  • ✅ Lead with a number or a sharp claim
  • ✅ Tell real deal stories (anonymised)
  • ✅ Use ₹ and Indian examples
  • ✅ Teach something usable in every post
  • ✅ Short lines. White space. One idea.
  • 🚫 Corporate buzzwords ("synergies", "ecosystem")
  • 🚫 Hype with no substance
  • 🚫 Investment advice, return promises, or guarantees
  • 🚫 Walls of text
  • 🚫 Emoji spam (1–3 max, purposeful)
⚖️ Compliance reflex: never promise returns or give investment advice. Frame as "a marketplace + tools", not "advice". Add "not investment advice" on anything that discusses numbers/valuation.
