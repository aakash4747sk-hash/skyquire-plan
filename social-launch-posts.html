③ Pillar: how to sell / exit (seller side)
LinkedIn"Thinking of selling your business? Read this first."Week 2–3
Most Indian business owners sell to the first person who asks. And leave 30–50% on the table. If you're thinking of exiting, do these 5 things first: → Clean up your books (2–3 years, GST-matched) → Document what runs without you → Know your number — get a real valuation, not a guess → Don't reveal your identity until a buyer is serious → Talk to more than one buyer (competition = price) Selling a business you built is emotional. The process shouldn't be chaotic too. We're building Skyquire so owners can find serious, verified buyers — confidentially — and exit on their terms. Selling in the next year? DM me. 👇 ⚠️ Not financial advice.
Why it works: speaks to the under-served seller side, gives a concrete checklist, and the confidentiality line maps directly to your mediated model.
