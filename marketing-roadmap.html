Marketing Road Path — hype → launch Dec 19

Build a public audience on both sides of the marketplace (buyers + sellers), powered by a founder-led content engine, then convert the hype into a loud public launch on Dec 19.
Budget ₹1.5–5L Engine Founder-led Audience Buyers + Sellers Runway ~26 weeks
📈 The 4-phase path
P1 · Jun–Jul

Plant the flag

Foundation & audience
  • Set up founder brand (LinkedIn + X), lock the narrative
  • Start build-in-public posting
  • Waitlist + “skip the line” referral
  • Launch 2 WhatsApp communities (buyers / sellers)
  • Cornerstone SEO posts (EN + Hindi)
  • Lead magnets: free valuation + health-score teaser
→ 500 waitlist · 2k founder followers
P2 · Aug–Sep

Build the audience

Content engine & community
  • Consistent cadence: LinkedIn 4–5×/wk, X daily, weekly video
  • Dual content: how to buy / how to sell a business
  • 20 CA & broker partnerships + co-webinars
  • First micro-creator collabs
  • Email nurture from lead magnets
  • Build-in-public: every shipped feature = a post
→ 2,000 waitlist · active community
P3 · Oct–Nov

Create the wave

Hype & scarcity
  • Paid acquisition: LinkedIn (corp) + Meta (buyers/sellers) + retargeting
  • Creator sponsorships (entrepreneur YouTubers)
  • PR outreach: YourStory, Inc42, ET, Mint
  • Founding-member program + “first 100 listings free”
  • Closed beta to waitlist · collect testimonials
  • 30-day countdown begins
→ 5,000 waitlist · beta + PR lined up
P4 · Dec 15–21

Go loud

Launch week · peak Dec 19
  • Daily countdown content
  • Launch-day blast: email + WhatsApp + LinkedIn/X + PR live
  • Product Hunt / launch platforms + live demo
  • Creator posts + press same day
  • Launch offers + referral incentives
→ Public launch · first paying users

🔥 Hype mechanics & budget

How the hype compounds

  • 🪜 Waitlist referral — “skip the line” & unlock perks for inviting friends
  • 🏅 Founding-member badges + lifetime perks for the first 500
  • 🎁 “First 100 listings free” — pulls in seller supply
  • 🔒 Limited beta seats — exclusivity drives FOMO
  • ⏳ 30-day public countdown to Dec 19
  • 🛠️ Build-in-public milestone reveals keep momentum between phases

Budget allocation (~₹3L mid)

PR & outreach12%

Front-load tools + organic in P1–P2 (cheap), shift weight to creators + paid + PR in P3–P4 when the wave needs amplifying.
